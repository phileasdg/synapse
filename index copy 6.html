How to Use

  • ▸

    Add Node: Click an empty area on the canvas.

  • ▸

    Connect/Disconnect: Click one node, then another. Press Esc to cancel connecting.

  • ▸

    Edit Node: Double-click a node.

  • ▸

    Set Layout Root: Right-click a node for Tree or Radial layouts.

  • ▸

    Pan & Zoom: Drag the canvas or use your scroll wheel.
